Brief Bio

Xiuwen Yi is currently a Data Scientist of JD Intelligent Cities Business Unit, focuses on using big data and AI technology to tackle urban challenges. He got his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Southwest Jiaotong University in 2018. He was an intern in Urban Computing Group at MSR Asia from 2014 to 2017. His research interests include: Spatiotemporal Data Mining, Deep Learning, and Urban Computing. He has published over 15 research papers in refereed conferences (e.g., KDD, IJCAI) and journals (e.g., EEE TKDE, AI). Also, he is experienced in building real-world applications of AI technology.
